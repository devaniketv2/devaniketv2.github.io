The Platform

Everything runs on your machines

📚

Knowledge collections & RAG

Upload documents into collections and get grounded, cited answers — hybrid vector + keyword retrieval with reranking, tuned per collection.

🧠

Document intelligence

Structured field extraction from quotes and invoices, side-by-side document comparison, and grounded drafting of new documents from your own corpus.

🎙️

Chat with voice — bilingual

Speak or type in English or Arabic. Local Whisper transcription, natural read-aloud voices, full RTL interface — no cloud round-trips.

🕸️

Knowledge graph

Entities and relationships extracted from your documents fuse with retrieval, so relational questions get relational answers.

🔌

Service API for machines

Other applications consume the AI engine over a versioned API with scoped keys — extraction, comparison, and drafting as a service.

🔒

Private by architecture

Models run locally through Ollama. Your documents, embeddings, and conversations stay in your own database, on your own hardware.
